A Word From The Khatib

"All Praise is due to Allah Alone, we Praise Him and Ask for his help and guidance. I bear witness "there is no god but Allah Alone, and I bear witness that Muhammad (PBUH) is his last Prophet and Messenger."

Assalamu'alay-kum (Peace be upon you!).

Welcome to our newborn website of the Bhuiyan Para Jaameh Mosque. Our Mosque serves as a symbol of the roots of Islam in this locality.
The Mosque is in Nizkunjara, Feni, only 500 meters away from the Dhaka-Chittagong highway, where hospitality, generosity, and tolerance are well known facts. It always welcomes you. It stands today as a piece of history and a landmark; it also symbolizes a new openness, as it opens its doors to all those who are seeking knowledge about Islam and Muslims across the world, about the Arab world and its culture, or general information about the Middle East.
On behalf of this Mosque, I welcome all of you to visit and explore the mosque that was built for Muslims of this locality. We hope that you can visit us or call upon us for all of your spiritual and intellectual needs.

Khatib
